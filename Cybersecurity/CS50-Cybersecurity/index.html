        • Dictionary words.
        • Repetitive or sequential characters (e.g. 'aaaaaaaa', '1234abcd').
        • Context-specific words, such as the name of the service, the username, and derivatives thereof.
      • Memorized secrets verifiers SHALL NOT permit the subescriber to store a "hint" that is accessible to an unauthenticated claimant. Verifiers SHALL NOT prompt subescribers to use specific types of information (e.g., "What was the name of your first pet?") when choosing memorized secrets.
      • Verifiers SHOULD NOT require memorized secrets to be changed arbitrary (e.g. periodically).
      • Verifiers SHALL implement a rate-limiting mechanism that effectively limits the number of failed authentication attempts that can be made on the subscriber's account.
